Background

Who I am and how I work

My background includes experience in areas connected to advisory work, investment and real estate brokerage, as well as exposure to demanding business environments. That foundation helped me develop a more analytical, structured and decision-oriented approach.

Today I apply that foundation to real estate, helping owners, buyers and investors better frame each step, from market reading to the actual closing of the deal.

What I aim to bring to each process

  • Market reading with stronger context
  • Commercial positioning with more coherence
  • Better preparation and presentation
  • Negotiation handled with discipline
  • Close support until completion
Principles

Transparency, responsibility and discretion

Transparency

A serious relationship starts with clarity. The client should understand the context, the options and the reasoning behind each decision.

Responsibility

Every property and every process has real consequences. The work should be carried out with method, discipline and awareness of what is at stake.

Confidentiality

Not every property and not every client should be handled in the same way. Discretion is part of serious representation that fits the context.

Clients

Who I work with

I work with owners, buyers and investors who value an informed, professional and structured approach.

I support clients who want to sell with more control, buy with more context or invest with a more rational reading of the asset, the location and the market.
