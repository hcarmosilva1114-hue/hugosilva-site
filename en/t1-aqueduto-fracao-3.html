Description

Historic heritage and contemporary architecture in the same setting

In Vila do Conde, some locations are not merely points on a map. They are lived. In this development, the Aqueduct becomes part of the daily setting and gives the property a rare identity with strong patrimonial appeal.

This ground-floor one-bedroom unit is part of a private contemporary condominium in the city centre, combining accessibility, comfort and construction quality. The immediate proximity to the Aqueduct reinforces the property’s singular character and future appreciation potential.

The interior layout is functional and balanced, with a modern living and kitchen concept, a well-sized bedroom and contemporary finishes. The ground-floor position adds convenience, easier access and strong appeal for owner-occupation or rental demand.
