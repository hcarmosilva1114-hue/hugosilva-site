Description

Space, versatility and income potential in one of Vila do Conde’s most sought-after areas

This house is located in Caxinas, a consolidated and highly sought-after area of Vila do Conde, close to the beach, Portas Fronhas metro station and with quick access to the A28 motorway.

Set on a 280 sqm plot, with 152 sqm of footprint and 252 sqm of gross construction area, it offers scale, versatility and a very interesting setting for both owner-occupation and investment.

The main five-bedroom house offers generous areas, good distribution and complementary spaces that reinforce everyday functionality. Added to this is an independent one-bedroom unit, especially relevant for immediate rental income or family support use.
