Before selling

There are three things that matter right from the beginning

Market positioning

Not all properties compete in the same way. Location, typology, condition, supply context and buyer profile shape the positioning.

Value protection

Entering the market badly can create noise, weak visits and reduce the property’s negotiating strength.

Exposure strategy

Some properties benefit from broader visibility. Others require more control, better filtering and a more deliberate commercial approach.

What you receive

A more useful first reading than just an isolated number

The initial review helps frame the property more clearly and understand what commercial approach makes more sense in light of the market, the location and your objective.

  • Location context
  • Commercial positioning
  • Comparable competition
  • Value perception
  • Market-entry logic
  • Possible next steps

This review is especially useful if

  • You are considering selling and want a better reading of the market
  • You have doubts about value and positioning
  • You want to avoid a disorganised market entry
